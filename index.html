Here we aim to optimise a cobb douglas utility function given a budget constraint the budget constraint.

Mathematically this can be written as

Maxamise A * xayb such that p1x +p2y = m

Where A, a, b are constants and so are p1 - the price for good x and p2 - the price for good y and m - income.

Note here we are assumming that the budget is satisified with equality (non satiation requirement is fulfiled) hence the constraint ( p1x +p2x = m) holds with equality.
